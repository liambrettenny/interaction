Inspirational Creatives

  • Denis Villeneuve
    Denis Villeneuve is a French Canadian film director and writer. He is best known for his feature films Arrival, Dune , Sicario, Prisoners, Enemy, and Blade Runner 2040.
  • Roger Deakins
    Roger Deakins is a cinematographer best known for his work on the films of the Coen brothers, Sam Mendes, and Denis Villeneuve.
  • Jony Ive
    Jony Ive is a industrial/Product designer who, while holding various posts at Apple Inc. (1992–2019), made design as integral to the appeal of a personal computer as its power and speed.
  • Erik Spiekermann
    Erik Spiekermann is a type designer who has built identities for Audi, Volkswagen and Bosch, and his hugely successful redesign of The Economist.
  • Es Devlin
    Es Devlin is an artist and stage designer. She is known for creating large-scale performative sculptures and environments that fuse music, language and light.
  • Tinker Hatfield
    Tinker Hatfield is one of the world's most legendary sneaker designers, and currently Nike's Vice President for Design and Special Projects.
  • Ian Spelter
    Ian Spalter is Head of Design at Instagram, where he leads the team responsible for all things design ranging from cross-platform app experiences to brand & identity.
  • Jonathan Hoefler
    Jonathan Hoefler is a typeface designer and an armchair type historian who specializes in the design of original typefaces.
  • Jacqueline Casey
    Graphic designer Jacqueline Casey is best known for the posters she created for MIT.
  • Akiko Stehrenberger
    Stehrenberger is a designer who has worked on projects for some of cinema’s most important and influential filmmakers, translating their unique vision from screen to film poster.
